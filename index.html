Recover Solana assets from compromised wallets

Has your wallet been compromised?

Our tool allows to try to get assets for those assets (tokens, nfts, etc) that are still tied to your compromised wallet, without needing to spend SOL from that wallet.

Safe wallet setup

In order to use this tool, you'll have to setup a second wallet app/extension, such as Phantom, Solflare, Backpack, ME wallet, etc.

Create yourself a NEW SEED for your safe wallet and add it to a different wallet app than were you have the compromised one. Then fund your safe wallet with some SOL.

What now?

Go to Account page and connect both wallets to start moving your assets out of your compromised wallet.
